v0.1.1
- One-shot DarkSubs handshake: when the user sets a Gemini key,
  flip DarkSubs's auto_translate off so its built-in Google/Bing
  Hebrew rows stop competing with (and outranking) ours. Gated
  so we never re-stomp on a user who deliberately toggled it
  back on.
- Fix: Clear-cache / Clear-temp buttons were hidden because an
  empty `label=""` on a sibling visible=false setting bailed Kodi
  21 out of rendering the rest of the group. Now label="0".
- Prompt: dedicated GENDER AGREEMENT section with concrete
  Hebrew examples for 1st/2nd/3rd person, and an explicit
  "double-check before finalising" instruction. Should reduce
  the occasional male/female mix-ups.
